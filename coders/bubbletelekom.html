Bubble Telekom

 

In words of their owners, Bubble Telekom is a young but powerful Internet Service Providers that offers Hosting, Housing, Bandwidth, system development, software engineering, global consulting to more than 100000 clients along the country. With last investments they hope to extend their high level professional service worldwide Within 6 months they supose that they will open a session in NYSE.

But times are changing the rules and the corporation image as well. It is in the hands of an aggressive marketing director: he has designed an amazing campaign that breaks all the rules of advertising and the rules that conform the common sense.

 

"Believe me. This new campaign will break into potential customers minds"
 
"I ensure that my desing will become a must know reference. There is no doubt about It"
 
"I can smell our future costumer's money, I can even taste the cocaine of dollar edges"
