Author

The author (me) is currently working at IBERCOM, a ISP from Spain. He has been in many sites working as a programmer or system admin; also, as any computer geek he has spent many hours in front of the screen on his own. So the situations and characters of the comics are more or less inspired in the real life.

He has a degree in Computer Engineering, and he studied in EHU-UPV. He spent 7 long years in the faculty, and at last once he finished the project (about jini technology) and after he passed AI exam (9/10) he became an engineer!!
