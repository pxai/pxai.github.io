Links

There are thousand comic strips available in Internet for free to see or even receive by email. Some of them are GNU licensed, (like this!) so you can distribute or copy them but always referencing the authorship.

USE with CAUTION: If you discover a good one you'll pass hours in front of your screen; try to consume them one by one, and daily if possible. The only overdose you need to live is caffeine.

I would like to present some comic strips, surely you recognize most of them:

  • ECOL: linux geek vs M$ supporter
  • User Friendly: think geek, my friend
  • Hackles: animals working in high-tec
  • Dilbert: a classical one
  • Liberty Meadows: a great strip you must discover

If you are developing a regular comic strip like this, don't forget to send the reference to me.
